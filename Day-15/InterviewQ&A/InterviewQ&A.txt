✅ Q1. Why is QuickSort better than Bubble Sort?

It divides the array into parts and sorts them recursively — fewer comparisons.

✅ Q2. What is a Binary Search Tree?

A hierarchical data structure where left < root < right.

✅ Q3. What is the time complexity of QuickSort?

Average: O(n log n)

Worst: O(n²)

✅ Q4. What is recursion?

A function calling itself to solve smaller parts of a problem.

✅ Q5. How is a tree different from a linked list?
Tree	Linked List
Hierarchical	Linear
Multiple children	Single next pointer
